Overview

Purpose

Most of my customers are travelers who've come to visit the local state park and end up hearing about my guiding services from my town's residents (via word-of-mouth). The purpose of this site is to create another avenue where vacationers who are researching entertainment and/or activities close to the state park can learn about my services. This will widen my customer base to include individuals who would otherwise not learn about my services from the locals.

Audience

My website will specifically target males, between the age of 35-45, who are financially resourceful enough to be able to afford an extended vacation that would include additional expenses such as recreational activities. These individuals want to step away from their daily routines to experience the excitement and thrill white water rafting has to offer. I anticipate these individuals will be planning out their vacation meticulously to leverage every second of it by researching activities and planning their vacation hour by hour on their laptops.
